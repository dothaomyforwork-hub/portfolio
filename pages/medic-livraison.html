Project Overview

Medic Livraison is a driver-focused healthcare delivery platform designed to streamline medication pickup, delivery, and return operations.

Built for real-world pharmacy logistics, the platform supports route management, cold-chain handling, proof-of-delivery, cash collection, and return workflows while ensuring operational accuracy.

The Challenge

Healthcare deliveries involve strict handling requirements, time-sensitive orders, and complex return scenarios. Drivers need clear guidance while staying compliant with operational procedures.

Our Approach

  • Simplified complex delivery workflows into guided task-based experiences.
  • Designed location-aware interactions to reduce operational errors.
  • Structured delivery, return, and payment flows around real driver behaviors.

Design Insights

Designing for Healthcare Operations in Motion

Healthcare deliveries involve far more than transportation. Drivers must manage urgent medications, refrigerated products, proof-of-delivery requirements, payment collection, and return procedures while working under strict time constraints.

At the same time, real-world operations rarely follow the ideal path. Orders may not be ready for pickup, patients may be unavailable, deliveries may fail, and returned items must be handled according to operational protocols.

The experience was designed to help drivers navigate both routine tasks and unexpected situations with confidence.

    Design Priorities

  • Urgent & Time-Critical Deliveries — Support priority orders without disrupting route efficiency
  • Cold-Chain Compliance — Highlight refrigerated medications at critical handling points
  • Exception-Driven Workflows — Guide drivers through failed deliveries, returns, and pickup issues
  • Operational Reliability — Reduce errors through validations, confirmations, and location-aware interactions
