Project Overview

PeakPlus is an AI-powered athlete performance platform that helps users optimize training, recovery, nutrition, and cognitive performance in one place.

The Challenge

Athletes often rely on fragmented tools for training, recovery, nutrition, and mental performance. The challenge was creating a unified experience that transforms complex performance data into clear actions without overwhelming users.

Our Approach

  • Centralized performance tracking across multiple domains
  • Designed AI-powered recommendations and insights
  • Built dedicated experiences for athletes and coaches
  • Simplified complex metrics through visual dashboards

Design Insights

Engaging the Learner

We found that adding small micro-interactions and progress tracking features significantly boosted daily active usage.

  • Gamified Progress — Visual milestones make studying feel rewarding.
  • Distraction-Free UI — A clean layout helps maintain focus during study sessions.
