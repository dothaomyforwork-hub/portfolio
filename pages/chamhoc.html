Project Overview

Cham Hoc is an educational platform created to support students preparing for Vietnam's competency assessment exams.

The platform brings together learning resources, mock exams, progress tracking, and community interaction into a structured learning ecosystem.

The Challenge

Research uncovered multiple pain points across different stages of exam preparation. Some students lacked a clear learning roadmap, others struggled with fragmented tools and resources, while many first-time candidates felt overwhelmed by unfamiliar exam requirements.

The challenge was to transform these disconnected needs into a cohesive learning ecosystem that supports students before, during, and throughout their preparation process.

Our Approach

  • Progress dashboards
  • Mock exam experience
  • Community support
  • Resource recommendations

Design Insights

Designing for Direction, Not Just Learning

Research revealed that many students were not lacking effort—they were lacking clarity. The experience was designed to help users understand what to learn, why it matters, and how they are improving over time.

  • Goal-Oriented Learning — Connect study activities with measurable outcomes.
  • Progress Visibility — Make improvement tangible through dashboards and learning analytics.

Process Highlights

Design Thinking in Practice

1

Empathize

Conducted surveys, interviews, empathy mapping, and persona development to understand students' motivations, challenges, and preparation behaviors.

2

Define

Synthesized research findings to identify key pain points, including unclear learning paths, fragmented resources, and limited exam understanding..

3

Ideate

Explored solutions through user stories, competitive audits, information architecture, and experience planning to shape a cohesive learning ecosystem.

4

Prototype

Created sitemap, user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity interfaces to visualize and validate the proposed experience.

5

Test

Conducted usability tests with real users to gather feedback and iterate on the design.

Key Takeaway

Clarity Creates Confidence

Students often struggle not because they lack effort, but because they lack direction. Designing Cham Hoc reinforced how thoughtful UX can transform uncertainty into confidence through structure, guidance, and meaningful feedback.
