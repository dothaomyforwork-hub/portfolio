Project Overview

My Spa Pro is a marketplace and management platform built for beauty and wellness professionals. It helps clients discover trusted providers while enabling businesses to manage appointments, services, and customer interactions in one place.

The Challenge

Unlike traditional booking platforms, most appointment actions required approval from both clients and providers. Booking requests, schedule changes, cancellations, and payment-related updates all depended on mutual confirmation, creating complexity across the entire service journey.

Without careful design, users could easily lose track of pending actions, approval status, and next steps.

Our Approach

  • Made approval status visible throughout the booking lifecycle.
  • Reduced uncertainty with clear action ownership and notifications.
  • Designed dedicated workflows for both clients and providers.
  • Simplified booking, modification, and confirmation processes into predictable steps.

Design Insights

Calm from the First Click

TBooking beauty services should feel as relaxing as the service itself. The experience was designed around clarity, trust, and simplicity to reduce decision fatigue and encourage confident bookings.

  • Service Discovery — Easy exploration across beauty and wellness categories.
  • Simplified Booking — Fewer steps from search to confirmation.
  • Trust Signals — Ratings, profiles, and service details support decision-making.
