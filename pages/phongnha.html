Project Overview

Commissioned by the Management Board of Phong Nha – Ke Bang National Park, this Zalo Mini App was designed to help visitors discover attractions, tours, events, dining options, and essential services through a seamless mobile experience.

The Challenge

Tourism information was scattered across multiple sources, making it difficult for visitors to quickly discover relevant places, compare options, and navigate the destination with confidence.

Our Approach

  • Consolidated tourism services into one unified platform.
  • Prioritized location-aware experiences for on-site visitors.
  • Designed concise, mobile-friendly content structures for quick decision-making.

Design Insights

Discover Faster, Explore Better

Visitors often make decisions while traveling, with limited time and attention. The experience was designed to surface the most relevant information quickly through proximity-based discovery, simplified navigation, and concise content presentation.

  • Context-Aware Discovery — Prioritizing nearby attractions and services.
  • Mobile-First Experience — Optimized for quick interactions on the go.

Key Takeaway

Designing for Visitors in Motion

Travelers need information that is fast, relevant, and easy to act on. This project reinforced the importance of simplifying complex tourism ecosystems into clear, location-aware experiences.
