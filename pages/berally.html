Project Overview

Berally is a next-generation decentralized social trading platform built on blockchain infrastructure. It bridges retail traders with AI-powered strategies, enabling peer-to-peer portfolio management and automated investment opportunities. The platform combines DeFi efficiency with social engagement, allowing users to create, follow, and profit from trading strategies.

The Challenge

Crypto trading platforms often overwhelm users with complex data, fragmented workflows, and technical terminology. For newcomers, identifying trustworthy strategies can be difficult. For experienced traders, sharing performance and attracting followers requires additional tools outside the platform.

The challenge was designing a product that balances transparency, trust, and usability while supporting real-time market activity.

Our Approach

We focused on creating a streamlined social trading experience centered around three key pillars:

  • Discover profitable trading opportunities.
  • Follow trusted traders and strategies.
  • Monitor portfolio performance in real time.

Design Insights

Visual Language & Strategy

Berally's visual identity was designed to communicate trust, transparency, and technological sophistication.

  • Dark-first experience — A dark interface reduces visual fatigue and aligns with user expectations within crypto products.
  • Data-focused hierarchy — Information grouped by task, allowing users to focus on what matters most for their current action
  • Strategic accent colors — Blue and purple accents guide attention toward actionable data, performance metrics, and key interactions.
  • Modular component system — Reusable cards, buttons, and widgets ensuring consistency across 12+ screens and future product expansions

Key Takeaway

Balancing Creativity and Feasibility

One of the biggest challenges during this project was aligning ambitious design ideas with technical and development constraints. Through close collaboration with developers, multiple design iterations, and continuous refinement, we were able to simplify complex concepts into practical solutions without losing the product's visual identity. This experience strengthened my ability to design with both creativity and feasibility in mind.
